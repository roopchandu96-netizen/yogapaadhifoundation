About the Foundation

Our Journey Towards Wellness

Yoga Paadhi Foundation was founded by Gali Tejaswi, a Certified Professional Yoga Teacher, with a deep-seated vision: to make the transformative power of authentic yoga accessible to everyone. We bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern wellness demands.

Our mission is to support individuals on their path to physical strength, mental clarity, and spiritual growth, establishing a lifestyle of balance and peace.
